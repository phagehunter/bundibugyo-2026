The Filovirus Proteome: Expected Divergence

Alignment-Free Similarity (Whole Genome K-mer Analysis)

To bypass the cross-species alignment issue, we compute a Jaccard Similarity Index using 9-mers (9-nucleotide fragments). This checks how many DNA fragments the viruses share globally, regardless of where they are positioned in the genome.
